About Us

Welcome to Blue Mermaid

Imagine waking to the sound of waves and walking straight to the beach — Blue Mermaid Homestay sits close to Thottada backwater and Thottada Beach, offering beachfront cottages, traditional-style homes and a relaxed, unhurried atmosphere. Located about 8 km from Kannur city, our homestay blends traditional local architecture with warm hospitality and coastal cuisine.
